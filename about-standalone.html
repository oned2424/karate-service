Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions from our community

Is it suitable for complete beginners?
Perfect for those new to karate

Absolutely! Complete beginners can start with confidence. Our detailed videos cover the fundamentals from the very beginning, allowing you to progress at your own comfortable pace.

Can I use it on my smartphone?
Works on all your devices

Yes, you can access Karate Pocket Dojo on smartphones, tablets, computers, and any device with internet access.

Can I access it from anywhere in the world?
Global access available

Yes, you can access our platform from anywhere in the world. We're also expanding multilingual support to serve our global community better.

What is the mirror function?
Learn like looking in a mirror

The mirror function flips the video horizontally, so you can practice facing the same direction as the instructor. This makes it much easier to learn movements accurately, just like practicing in front of a mirror.

How does slow motion playback work?
Master complex movements step by step

You can play complex movements and techniques at 0.5x speed. This allows you to examine every detail of the motion and develop proper form with precision.
